== Description ==



Name Your Price for WooCommerce is a powerful and versatile plugin that allows customers to set their own prices for products or services on your WooCommerce store. This tool helps you create a personalized shopping experience and foster greater engagement with your customers.

== Using Name Your Price for WooCommerce for Donation Products ==
Name Your Price for WooCommerce is an excellent solution for creating donation products in WooCommerce. This plugin allows customers to enter their own donation amount, making it easier for businesses and nonprofits to receive contributions without being restricted by fixed pricing.

== Flexible Pricing ==
Customers can enter the amount they are willing to pay for a product or service.

== Set Minimum and Maximum Prices ==
Ensure pricing aligns with your business strategy.

== Supports Various Product Types ==
Works seamlessly with simple products, downloadable products, variable products, and subscriptions.

== Customizable Interface ==
Easily adjust the input fields and messages to match your store’s design.

== Discount Strategy Support ==
Create unique promotional campaigns by letting customers feel empowered to decide the price.

== Detailed Reporting ==
Track and analyze customer behavior to optimize your pricing strategy.

== Boost Sales and Customer Satisfaction ==
Customers feel valued and engaged when they have control over pricing, increasing conversion rates.

== Test Pricing Strategies ==
Use this plugin to experiment with pricing and discover what customers are willing to pay.

== Ideal for Donations or Fundraising Products ==
Perfect for use in scenarios where customers can set their own price for charitable purposes.


Name Your Price for WooCommerce is an excellent choice to introduce creativity and flexibility into your business strategy. Easy to integrate, highly compatible, and designed to elevate your WooCommerce store’s customer experience!

== Installation ==
**Normal installation**

1. Download the woocommerce.zip file to your computer.
1. Unzip the file.
1. Upload the `plugin.zip` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
Document include in plugin
